About Us

Company overview

HERE I AM was founded in 2023 by Hlayisekani Mnisi, driven by a vision to revolutionize safety solutions and empower individuals with proactive measures.

The inspiration behind HERE I AM stemmed from the founder's personal experiences and a deep-seated commitment to address critical safety concerns, especially in light of escalating gender-based violence and other safety issues in South Africa.

The founding team, led by Hlayisekani Mnisi, comprises dedicated individuals with diverse backgrounds in technology, entrepreneurship, and social impact. Their collective passion for creating positive change and enhancing safety in communities has been instrumental in shaping HERE I AM's journey.

The Inspiration Behind HERE I AM: A Personal Journey



The genesis of HERE I AM traces back to a poignant moment in time, a moment that stirred a deep sense of responsibility and determination. It was a time when the echoes of tragedy reverberated through the nation, when the safety of women and vulnerable individuals was tragically compromised. The catalyst for HERE I AM was not just a singular event but a culmination of societal challenges, personal experiences, and an unwavering commitment to change. The incident that sparked the inception of HERE I AM was the heartbreaking murder of a pregnant woman, a crime that shook the community to its core. The woman, a symbol of life and hope, was found tragically hanging from a tree, a victim of senseless violence and disregard for human life.

In the midst of grief and outrage, the realization dawned that such tragedies were not isolated incidents but symptomatic of broader safety concerns. Gender-based violence had spiked, and the safety of women, children, and vulnerable individuals was increasingly at risk. It was a pivotal moment of introspection and determination—a moment that propelled the vision for HERE I AM.

As the founder of HERE I AM, I felt a profound sense of duty and indebtedness to my fellow women, to keep them as safe as I would want to be in my country, on my streets, in my community. The idea for HERE I AM fully crystallized—a discreet wearable device equipped with advanced tracking and emergency alert features, designed to empower individuals, especially women, with proactive safety measures. HERE I AM is not just a product; it's a testament to resilience, empowerment, and the unwavering belief that safety is a fundamental right. It's a tribute to those whose lives were tragically cut short, a promise to honor their memory by creating tangible solutions for a safer, more secure world. The journey of HERE I AM is fueled by a passion for change, a commitment to innovation, and a vision of a society where safety knows no boundaries. It's a journey we invite you to join, as together, we redefine safety and empower individuals to say, "HERE I AM, safe and empowered

Journey from Concept to Productive Development

Empowering Safety and Security for Everyone


In 2023, I embarked on a transformative journey by applying to the entrepreneur program with I'M IN. This marked the beginning of a path filled with challenges, learning opportunities, and moments of inspiration that would shape the creation of HERE I AM.

The journey commenced with rigorous phases and vetting processes, culminating in an achievement: making it to the stage of 150 entrepreneurs. This initial success was a testament to the potential and vision behind HERE I AM, fueling my determination to pursue this venture further. One pivotal moment in this journey was attending a workshop with female entrepreneurs who not only motivated me but also instilled a sense of purpose and dedication to the course. Their stories of resilience, innovation, and impact inspired me to stay committed and focused on creating a meaningful solution that addresses critical safety concerns.

The workshop also shed light on the unique role of women entrepreneurs in tackling societal challenges. Women entrepreneurs often bring a natural sense of nurture and empathy to their ventures, leading them to tackle sections of society, the economy, technology, or the environment that others may overlook. Empowered by these insights and driven by a passion for change, I continued to refine and develop the concept of HERE I AM. The vision was clear: to create a discreet wearable device equipped with advanced tracking and emergency alert features, designed to empower individuals with proactive safety measures.

Through dedication, perseverance, and collaborative efforts, HERE I AM evolved from a concept into a tangible reality. Making it into the round of 30 entrepreneurs marked a significant milestone in this journey. HERE I AM is now poised for productive development, ready to make a meaningful impact on safety and security in our communities.

This journey from concept to productive development for HERE I AM is a testament to the power of determination, inspiration, and collaboration. It's a journey of turning challenges into opportunities, learning from experiences, and staying true to a vision that seeks to empower individuals and create positive change.
